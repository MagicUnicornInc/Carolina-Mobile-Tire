What areas does Carolina Tire serve? +

We proudly serve multiple locations across the Southeast. Our mobile tire service is available in Charleston, SC and Knoxville, TN. We have physical shop locations in Powell, TN, Lenoir City, TN, and Rocky Mount, NC. Each location offers a unique set of services tailored to meet local needs.

What brands of tires do you carry? +

We stock a comprehensive selection of major tire brands including Michelin, Goodyear, Bridgestone, Continental, BFGoodrich, and many more. We also offer quality inspected used tires for budget-conscious customers. Our experts will help you find the perfect tire for your vehicle, driving style, and budget.

Do you offer warranties on your services? +

Yes! We stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties. All new tires come with manufacturer warranties, and we offer additional road hazard protection. Our labor is backed by our service guarantee. Specific warranty terms vary by service and location - please ask our technicians for complete details.

How long have you been in business? +

Carolina Tire has been serving the Southeast for decades. With decades of experience, we've built our reputation on honest work, fair prices, and exceptional customer service. We're a family-owned business that has grown from a single location to multiple shops and mobile service units.

How does mobile tire service work? +

Our mobile tire service is simple and convenient. You call us or book online, choose a time and location that works for you, and our fully equipped service van comes to you. Our certified technician will perform the tire service on-site using professional equipment. You can be at home, work, or even stranded on the roadside - we come to you!

What services can you perform mobile? +

Our mobile units can handle tire installation, tire repairs, tire rotation, flat tire fixes, and emergency roadside assistance. We carry state-of-the-art equipment for mounting, balancing, and repairing tires on-site. For more complex automotive repairs, we'll recommend visiting one of our shop locations.

Is mobile service more expensive? +

Our mobile service is competitively priced with a small convenience fee. When you factor in the time and gas you save by not driving to a shop and waiting, many customers find it's actually more economical. We provide upfront pricing with no hidden fees.

What areas do you cover for mobile service? +

Our mobile tire service currently operates in Charleston, SC and Knoxville, TN, covering these cities and surrounding areas within a 25-mile radius. We're constantly expanding our service areas, so please call to check if we can reach your location.

Do I need an appointment for shop service? +

While we welcome walk-ins, we recommend scheduling an appointment to minimize your wait time. Appointments ensure we have the right technicians and parts ready for your service. For emergencies, we always try to accommodate immediate needs.

What automotive services do you offer beyond tires? +

At our Powell, TN and Rocky Mount, NC locations, we offer comprehensive automotive services including brake repair, oil changes, engine diagnostics, transmission service, A/C repair, and more. Our technicians, including ASE Certified Technicians Available, can handle most automotive repair needs.

How long does a typical tire service take? +

Service times vary by type: Tire rotation takes about 30 minutes, mounting and balancing new tires takes 45-60 minutes for a set of four, and tire repair typically takes 30-45 minutes. We'll provide an accurate time estimate when you arrive.

Do you work on commercial vehicles? +

Yes! We service commercial vehicles including work trucks, vans, and light-duty commercial vehicles. We understand that downtime costs money, so we prioritize getting commercial customers back on the road quickly. Please call ahead for commercial service to ensure we have the right equipment and tires in stock.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and checks. For commercial accounts, we offer net payment terms. Our mobile service units are equipped with secure card readers for your convenience.

Do you offer financing options? +

Yes, we offer financing options for qualifying customers through our partner lenders. This can help spread the cost of new tires or major repairs over time. Ask our service advisors about current financing promotions and terms.

Do you price match competitors? +

We strive to offer competitive pricing on all our products and services. We're happy to discuss pricing options that work within your budget while maintaining our high service standards.

Are there any hidden fees? +

Absolutely not! We believe in transparent pricing. All costs including parts, labor, disposal fees, and taxes will be clearly explained before we begin any work. You'll always approve the final price before we proceed with service.

How quickly can you respond to emergencies? +

Our 24/7 emergency service typically responds within 30-60 minutes, depending on your location and current demand. We prioritize emergencies and dangerous situations. During peak times or severe weather, response times may be longer, but we'll keep you informed of our estimated arrival.

What constitutes a tire emergency? +

Tire emergencies include blowouts, severe punctures, sidewall damage, or any tire condition that makes driving unsafe. If you're stranded on the roadside, have multiple flat tires, or feel unsafe driving on your current tires, that's an emergency. When in doubt, call us - we'll help assess the situation.

Do you offer towing services? +

Yes, we offer towing services in the Knoxville, TN area. If your vehicle needs more than tire service or isn't safe to drive, we can tow it to one of our shop locations for comprehensive repairs. Towing fees vary by distance.

Is emergency service available on holidays? +

Yes! Our emergency roadside assistance operates 24/7/365, including all holidays. We understand that tire problems don't take holidays, and neither do we. Our emergency crews are always ready to help get you back on the road safely.
