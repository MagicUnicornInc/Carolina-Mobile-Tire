Our Journey Through Time

From a single shop to a regional leader in automotive services

The Beginning
Our Foundation
Carolina Tire opens its first location in Rocky Mount, NC with a commitment to honest work and fair prices.
1975
Expansion Era
Growing beyond tires, we begin offering comprehensive automotive services to better serve our customers.
1990
Tennessee Expansion
We expand into Tennessee, opening locations in Knoxville, Powell, and Lenoir City.
2010
South Carolina Arrival
Carolina Tire enters South Carolina, bringing our services to Charleston and the Lowcountry.
2020
Mobile Innovation
Launch of our revolutionary mobile tire service, bringing convenience directly to customers' locations.
Today
Future Forward
Continuing our legacy while embracing new technologies and expanding our mobile services.
